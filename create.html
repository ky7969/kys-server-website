Ky's Create SMP

This is my first distributed modpack creation, based around Create and the new Aeronautics addon. I have also included various optimization mods, QOL mods, ComputerCraft, world generation mods, more mobs, vanilla+ tools, backpacks, shops, hats, a map, land claiming, Litematica, building blocks, and more. Due to the addition of Aeronautics, I have nerfed the Elytra to encourage building vehicles. To play on the server, you will have to install the modpack on your local device. I have included a guide using Prism Launcher, but the pack can be installed into other third-party launchers. This pack will not work with the normal Minecraft launcher.

Installing the Modpack

1. Install Prism Launcher
Prism launcher is a free, open-source, third-party Minecraft launcher. It is typically used to install/create modpacks, however it is much better than the first-party Minecraft launcher, even for non-modded(vanilla) applications. As stated above, you can use other third-party launchers to install this pack, just not the first-party launcher.
Prism Launcher Download and Installation
2. Log In to Prism Launcher With Your Microsoft Account.
3. Add a New Instance.
Click "Add Instance" near the top left of the screen.
4. Select "Import" near the left side of the menu. Don't worry about naming the instance.
5. Paste the download link into the download field.
LINK BOX PLACEHOLDER
6. Click "OK" near the bottom right of the window.
7. Wait for the installation to complete.
8. All done! Double-click the instance to launch it. You can also select the instance near the right of the window.
